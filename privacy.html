Privacy Policy

SiRa Convert is designed for safe document workflows with browser-first processing and transparent policy practices.

Most file processing is performed directly in your browser.
We focus on minimal data collection for product analytics and reliability.
You control what you upload, process, and keep on your device.

1. Data We Process

Files selected in conversion tools are processed by app logic to generate outputs. File names and metadata may be handled temporarily in browser memory while you use a tool.

2. Local Processing

Core conversion actions are intended to run client-side. This architecture is designed to reduce unnecessary transfer of your document content.

3. Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand page traffic and product quality. This does not change your control over documents you process in the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers may process standard technical logs.
  • Authentication providers may apply their own privacy practices when sign-in is used.
  • Third-party services are governed by their own terms and policies.

5. Retention and Security

Temporary processing data should not be retained longer than needed for app function. You remain responsible for secure storage and handling of exported files.

6. Policy Owner

This app and policy are managed by RAGHAV PRATAP. Policy updates may occur as legal or product requirements change.
